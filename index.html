FAQ

Is my data private?
Yes. LocalChat runs entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers unless you explicitly enable optional network tools.

Do I need an internet connection?
Only to download models. After that, everything works fully offline.

Which devices are supported?
LocalChat works on iPhones and iPads running iOS 17 or later.
