Privacy Policy

LocalChat

Effective date: February 7, 2026

Overview

LocalChat is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app runs AI models entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or conversations.

Data That Stays on Your Device

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • Conversations and messages
  • AI model files
  • Memories and summaries
  • Document attachments
  • App settings and preferences
  • Voice recordings (processed on-device, never stored)

Optional Network Features

LocalChat includes optional network tools that are disabled by default. If you choose to enable them, the following third-party services may be contacted:

  • Apily: Search queries are sent to the Apily API to retrieve results.
  • Weather (wttr.in): Location names you provide are sent to wttr.in to retrieve weather data.
  • Wikipedia: Search terms are sent to the Wikipedia API to retrieve article summaries.

These features only activate when you explicitly enable network tools in Settings. No data is sent to these services otherwise.

Model Downloads

When you download an AI model, the app connects to HuggingFace to retrieve the model file. No personal information is sent during this process. After download, the model runs entirely offline.

Speech Recognition

LocalChat offers voice input via Apple's Speech Recognition framework. You can choose between:

  • On-device recognition (default): All processing happens locally. No audio data leaves your device.
  • Cloud recognition: If enabled, audio data is processed by Apple's servers under Apple's privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

LocalChat offers a Pro unlock via Apple's StoreKit. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or have access to your payment information.

Analytics and Tracking

LocalChat does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage of the app.

Children's Privacy

LocalChat does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
